Why Cyber Security?

I was first introduced to the world of cybersecurity through Sentinel in Secondary 2, where I learned the basics of cryptography and web development along with its real-world applications. Through this experience, I realised that cybersecurity extends far beyond creating strong passwords. There is an entire layer of complexity behind something as simple as a login page. This discovery sparked my interest in problem-solving, an area I have always enjoyed, especially when it involves thinking creatively. When I began coding, I further realised that there is often more than one way to solve a problem, and that some solutions are more effective than others. This became especially evident in cybersecurity, where experimenting with various challenges and developing my own solutions has been both engaging and rewarding.
