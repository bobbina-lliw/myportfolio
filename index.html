Why Cyber Security?

I started becoming interested in cybersecurity at the end of sec1 after watching the show "Mission Impossible-Dead Reckoning. I was fascinated by the abilities of the AI infiltrating digital space and having full control of anything online. This was when I started becoming interested in the digital world and its dangers. Later on I signed up for the sentinel program at the start of sec 2 and further enhanced my knowledge of cybersecurity. Through simple yet fun tasks given in sentinel I learned of real world uses of cybersecurity. For example behind a seemingly simple login page lies significant complexity, where passwords have to be hashed, inputs have to be sanitised to prevent any unauthorised access. Through sentinel I also found great joy and satisfaction experimenting and solving problems.
